About Me

I was born and raised in the North Texas area. I became interested in computers starting in the fourth grade. My elementary school had a TRS-80 that floated between classes on a cart. By fifth grade we had two TI-99/4A computers in the classroom.

My first program printed HELLO on the screen using BASIC.

10 Print "Hello"
20 Goto 10
Run

Now, I'm a system administrator managing over 200 servers and a couple of hunderd terabytes of storage.

I like to game in my spare time; either board games or computer games. I have an interest in photography but I haven't picked up my camera in years.

I'm hoping this class will lead to some new adventures.
